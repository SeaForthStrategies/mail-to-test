San Diego Leadership

Current leadership and what's happening in America's Finest City.

Leadership

Mayor Todd Gloria is San Diego's current mayor. He was re-elected in November 2024 and has served since December 2020. Gloria previously served as San Diego City Council President, Interim Mayor (2013), and California State Assembly member. In June 2025, he was elected Vice President of the United States Conference of Mayors, giving San Diego a stronger voice in advocating for America's cities.

What's Going On

Mayor Gloria released his Final Proposed Budget for Fiscal Year 2026, with reallocations to public safety and partial restoration of animal services funding. He has doubled down on major projects including the Civic Center redevelopment and Midway Rising, which aim to bring a new sports arena and transform downtown despite ongoing legal challenges.

A proposed ballot measure to tax vacant second homes has advanced—it would impose an annual tax on homes left unoccupied for more than 182 days, with revenue estimated between $12–30 million for housing and city services.
